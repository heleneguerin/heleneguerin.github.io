Hélène Guérin

Professor
Université du Québec à Montréal (UQAM)


I am professor at the Université du Québec à Montréal (UQAM) since 2019.
My research area is the study of stochastic processes, their properties and their long-time behavior. I am interested in models in various fields (physics, biology, actuarial science, etc.), involving processes such as piecewise deterministic Markov processes (PDMP), interacting particle systems, diffusions on manifold, random walks and Lévy processes.
I teach in the Actuarial Science, Statistics and Mathematics programs of the Mathematics Department.
